Why work at Artiea?

Working at ARTIEA means experiencing an international environment that fosters individual talent and encourages ongoing personal and professional development.

Being creative, collaborative, resilient, pragmatic, an effective communicator, inclusive, tireless in the pursuit of customer satisfaction, and a promoter of talent, learning and teaching on a daily basis, are the characteristics required to join the team, perfectly aligned with the company values of Sustainability, Excellence, and Integrity.

What we offer those who embrace our philosophy are real and concrete growth opportunities, closely linked to the tangible results achieved.

Our process
of selection

At Artiea, we want to offer you the best selection experience through a structured process that tells you who we are and provides you with all the information about the role you've applied for.

1. Apply online, remembering to attach your updated CV and, if you like, a short cover letter to help us understand who you are and why you're interested in the position.

2. If your application passes the pre-screening, the recruiter will contact you for a telephone interview to verify your suitability for the position and your ability to address the duties and challenges of the job.

3. If you are selected, you will undergo a selection process with the hiring managers, during which your compatibility with company values, motivation, strengths, and skills relevant to the role will be assessed.

We will evaluate your passion, skills, and motivation to join the Artiea family.
