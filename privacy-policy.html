Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 01, 2025

Artiea We Respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you visit our website, purchase our lighting products, or interact with us in any other way.

1. Scope and Purpose

This Policy applies to all users of our website, online shop, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, “Services”). By using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal and technical information to deliver premium experiences:

  • Personal Data: name, contact details, address, payment info, purchase history.
  • Technical Data: device type, browser, IP address, session activity.
  • Cookies: to personalize your lighting discovery journey and remember preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders securely.
  • To offer design inspirations and tailored lighting recommendations.
  • To communicate updates, offers, or warranty information.
  • To improve our website and customer service experience.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We share your data only with trusted partners—such as payment gateways, delivery services, or IT providers—solely to provide our services. We never sell personal data for advertising purposes.

5. International Transfers

As an Italian brand with a global presence, your data may be processed in other countries with appropriate data-protection safeguards consistent with GDPR standards.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill our purposes or comply with legal obligations, after which it is securely deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

8. Security

We employ encryption, secure servers, and limited access measures to protect your information. While no system is infallible, we continuously monitor and strengthen our safeguards.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 16 years old, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Policy periodically. Updates will appear on this page with a new “Last Updated” date. Major changes will be announced via email or site notice.
