About ARTIEA

Artiea is a visionary Italian lighting brand redefining the dialogue between light and architecture. With passion and precision, we craft lighting experiences that shape atmosphere, emotion, and expression. Each design reflects the timeless Italian spirit — minimalist, elegant, and purposeful.

Excellence in Every Detail

At Artiea, every design begins with curiosity and evolves with craftsmanship. We create lighting that enhances architecture, highlights form, and enriches human experience. The result is not just illumination — it’s emotion shaped by light.

Design and Development with Passion

Driven by innovation and refined aesthetics, our designers and engineers work together to explore new forms, materials, and technologies. Every Artiea piece captures a balance of art and science, crafted entirely in Italy for timeless elegance.

Sustainability Illuminated

Sustainability is more than a principle — it’s our purpose. From recyclable materials to energy-efficient production, we craft lighting that respects both people and planet. Each Artiea creation embodies longevity, responsibility, and refined beauty.

A Culture of Light and Collaboration

At Artiea, light begins with people. We nurture creativity and collaboration, empowering every member of our team to grow and innovate. Together, we build designs that inspire, connect, and illuminate the future of Italian lighting.
