About ARTIEA

Artiea is a visionary lighting brand dedicated to the art of illumination. We design and craft refined lighting solutions that bring architecture to life, transforming spaces into experiences. Each creation reflects our passion for precision, innovation, and timeless elegance.

Excellence Shaped by Light

At Artiea, quality is more than a promise — it’s a craft held in our hands. We create lighting that enhances how people live, work, and feel. From architectural luminaires to statement pendants, every piece begins its journey at our Italian studio, where design and production unite under one roof.

The Passion Behind Every Glow

Innovation, artistry, and technology drive Artiea’s design philosophy. Our creative and R&D teams constantly explore new materials and ideas, shaping light that blends functionality with emotion. Every fixture tells a story of balance — between precision and beauty.

Sustainability and Social Engagement

Artiea believes that innovation shines brightest when it respects the world around us. Every product is crafted with longevity, energy efficiency, and recyclability in mind. Our Italian production runs on renewable energy and embraces eco-conscious materials to protect the planet for future generations.

A Workplace That Inspires

Behind every Artiea creation is a team driven by passion and purpose. We cultivate an environment where creativity thrives, ideas flourish, and collaboration defines our success. At Artiea, hierarchy gives way to harmony — and people are at the heart of every light we make.
